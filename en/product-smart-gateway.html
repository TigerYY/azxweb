Overview

The Smart Gateway serves as the on-site access foundation for Power Grid IoT. It supports multi-protocol connectivity, data preprocessing and edge automation for substations, switching stations, distribution rooms, PV/storage and more. With local buffering and resume-on-reconnect, it keeps data complete and services continuous even under weak or disconnected networks‚Äîimproving integration efficiency and O&M reliability.
